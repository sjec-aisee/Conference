Call for Papers

AISEE welcomes paper submissions across six tracks listed below. Submissions may also cover relevant topics beyond those explicitly mentioned.

Track 1: Intelligent Systems, Vision & Language
  • Computer Vision and Image Understanding
  • Video Analytics and Intelligent Perception
  • Natural Language Processing and Understanding
  • Speech Recognition and Audio Intelligence
  • Multimodal Vision–Language Models
  • AI for Autonomous Systems
  • Affective Computing and Human–AI Interaction
  • Knowledge Representation and Reasoning
  • Explainable and Trustworthy Intelligent Systems
Track 2: AI in Healthcare
  • Medical Image and Signal Processing
  • AI for Disease Diagnosis
  • Clinical Decision Support Systems
  • AI in Personalized and Precision Medicine
  • Bioinformatics and Computational Biology
  • AI for Drug Discovery and Repurposing
  • Wearable Sensors and Remote Health Monitoring
  • AI in Mental Health and Behavioral Analysis
  • Federated Learning and Privacy-Preserving Healthcare AI
  • Ethical and Legal Aspects of AI in Healthcare
Track 3: AI for Smart and Secure Systems
  • AI-Enabled IoT Systems
  • Blockchain Technologies
  • Intelligent Network and Communication Systems
  • AI for Cybersecurity
  • Secure and Trusted AI Systems
  • Edge and Fog Computing
  • AI for Smart Cities
  • Secure Data Analytics
  • AI-Driven Automation for Industry 4.0
Track 4: Communication Systems
  • Analog Communication
  • Digital Communication
  • Wireless and Mobile Communication
  • 5G and Emerging 6G Technologies
  • Antenna Design and Propagation
  • RF and Microwave Engineering
  • Optical Fiber Communication
  • Satellite Communication
  • Radar Communication Systems
  • Communication System Modeling and Simulation
Track 5: Signal Processing & VLSI Systems
  • Digital Signal Processing Techniques
  • Filter Design and Spectral Analysis
  • Signal Analysis using Transform Techniques
  • Adaptive Filtering and Applications
  • DSP Algorithms and Architectures
  • VLSI Design and Implementation
  • FPGA-Based System Design
  • ASIC Design and Verification
  • System-on-Chip (SoC) Design
  • Embedded Systems and Real-Time Operating Systems
Track 6: Electrical Power and Energy Systems
  • Power Systems
  • Power Quality
  • Renewable Energy Systems
  • Energy Storage
  • Smart Grid
  • High Voltage Engineering
  • HVDC & FACTS
  • Power Electronics & Drives
  • Electric Transportation Systems
  • Instrumentation & Control
